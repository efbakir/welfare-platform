Typography & Spacing

Hero Heading

48–64px, weight 700, tight tracking, generous top/bottom space.

Section Title

28–36px, weight 700, short and scannable.

Body Copy

14–16px, muted secondary text, avoid dense paragraphs.

Rhythm

80–120px spacing between major sections; roomy cards and gutters.

Typeface: Plus Jakarta Sans for modern fintech readability and cleaner hierarchy.

Radius & Depth

Radius System

4 / 8 / 12 / 16 only. Cards use 16, interactive controls use 8.

Shadow System

Soft diffuse shadows: 0 8px 24px rgba(41,35,101,0.06) and up.

Glass Panel

rgba(255,255,255,0.82) + subtle border + 12px blur.

Component Style

Recommended
Frosted Feature Block Use subtle tint shifts and airy padding to separate content without heavy borders.

Usage Rules

Do

Use gradients for hero backgrounds, button emphasis, and highlighted states.

Do

Pair data blocks with soft tone differences (lavender/white) for scannability.

Don't

Avoid flat corporate blues, harsh black shadows, and dense dashboard clutter.
